Projects

Hello and welcome to my Projects! This is a collection of projects I have worked on in recent times. Most of the non-game projects I've worked on are visiable on my github page

Lucifer's Hordes is a rouge-like dungeon crawler. Where you use found weapons and skills to fight off Lucifer's hordes of demons. Developed in Unreal 4, me and 4 fellow class mates designed and developed this game over the course of 3 months. My primary contributions were handling item and enemy spawning, along with handling the weapon the player and enemies wielded. I also created the randomization of the dungeon layout as well as creating a sudo random spawning system for enemies.
